Class: Plant

Attributes:
plantName (string)
monthsOld (integer)
inchesTall (float)
fertilized (boolean)
watered (boolean)

Methods:
addFertilizer (changes fertilized attribute to true)
trim (subtracts a number from the inchesTall parameter)
label (displays string from plantName + displays an integer from monthsOld)
water (changes the watered attribute to true)
